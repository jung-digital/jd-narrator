STORY WORKSHOP

Most brands are telling a story, just not the right one.

Each Story Workshop includes:
  1. A brand review to assess
    • Your current story and how it can be improved
  2. A workbook of exercises to
    • Identify and define your brand personality and character
    • Reveal the challenges and obstacles to be resolved
    • Find the point of emotional leverage
    • Create a Story Plot to define what your brand story is all about
    • Develop a Story Message Map to guide how the story unfolds
  3. A framework bringing all marketing efforts around the core story
    • Draft and final versions of the brand Story Plot and Story Message Map
